ABOUT ME

My name is Humberto Navarro, I've been interested in computers and computer programming since I was 5 years old. It all started with a hot wheels PC that was released around 2004, 17 years later, I became a hobbyist programmer learning the ins and outs of software and game development.

My career in programming started with C#, in Unity 3D, which taught me to hate, but then evntually love Object Oriented Programming. This is my strongest technical skill thus far. Apart from this, I have extensive knowledge of the GNU/Linux ecosystem and command line. I also know many additional languages such as Javascript, Python, Java, C++, and PHP.

Technical skills aside, one of my greatest soft skills is the ability to take charge and oversee a vision. I have foresight when getting projects off the ground, knowledge of my own abilities, and of my coworkers. All important traits when I find myself in a leadership role, which happens all too often for me in technical situations, or on the sidelines deciding how to execute my work in a way that is best for the overall objective.
